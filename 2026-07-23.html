10 个实用词汇

卡片网格 · 含音标/例句/记忆提示
itinerary
/aɪˈtɪnəreri/
n.
行程安排，旅行计划
Could you send me the detailed itinerary for our trip to Tokyo?
💡 记忆：itin(走) + erary → 一路要走的东西 → 行程单
layover
/ˈleɪoʊvər/
n.
中转停留，转机时间
We have a three-hour layover in Dubai before flying to London.
💡 记忆：lay(停留)+over(越过) → 停留一下再越过 → 中转
accommodation
/əˌkɒməˈdeɪʃn/
n.
住宿，膳宿
The hotel provides free accommodation for conference speakers.
💡 记忆：ac + commod(方便) + ation → 提供方便的住处
departure
/dɪˈpɑːrtʃər/
n.
出发，离港（反：arrival）
The departure time of our flight has been delayed by two hours.
💡 记忆：de(离开)+part(部分)+ure → 离开 → 出发
arrival
/əˈraɪvl/
n.
到达，抵达
Upon arrival, please proceed to the baggage claim area.
💡 记忆：a(向)+riv(河岸)+al → 到达岸边 → 抵达
reservation
/ˌrezərˈveɪʃn/
n.
预订，预约
I'd like to make a reservation for two at 7 p.m.
💡 记忆：re(再)+serv(保留)+ation → 再次保留 → 预订
souvenir
/ˌsuːvənɪr/
n.
纪念品
She bought a small souvenir for each of her colleagues.
💡 记忆：音似"苏venir" → 记得买纪念品
cruise
/kruːz/
n./v.
乘船游览，游轮旅行
They went on a Mediterranean cruise for their honeymoon.
💡 记忆：音似"巡航" → 巡航游览 → 游轮
excursion
/ɪkˈskɜːrʒn/
n.
短途旅行，郊游
The company organized a day excursion to the nearby mountains.
💡 记忆：ex(出)+curs(跑)+ion → 跑出去 → 短途出游
transit
/ˈtrænzɪt/
n.
中转，公共交通
The package is currently in transit to your city.
💡 记忆：trans(跨越)+it(走) → 跨越行走 → 中转/运输

10 句情景对话

日常生活 + 职场场景
✈️ 场景 1 · 机场值机
A
Good morning. I'd like to check in for flight CA123 to Beijing.
早上好。我想办理去北京的CA123航班值机。
B
Sure. May I see your passport and booking reference?
好的。请出示您的护照和预订编号。
🏨 场景 2 · 酒店入住
A
Hi, I have a reservation under the name Li.
您好，我以"李"的名字预订了房间。
B
Welcome! Let me pull up your booking. You're in room 808.
欢迎！我查一下您的预订。您住808房间。
🗺️ 场景 3 · 问路
A
Excuse me, how do I get to the subway station?
打扰一下，地铁站怎么走？
B
Go straight and turn left at the second corner. It's on your right.
直走，在第二个拐角左转。就在您右手边。
🍽️ 场景 4 · 餐厅点餐
A
What do you recommend today?
今天您推荐什么？
B
The grilled salmon is our specialty. Would you like to try it?
烤三文鱼是我们的招牌。您想尝尝吗？
💼 场景 5 · 职场：安排出差
A
Could you help me book a flight to Shanghai for next Tuesday?
能帮我订下周二去上海的机票吗？
B
Of course. Do you prefer a morning or evening departure?
当然。您偏好上午还是晚上出发？
📞 场景 6 · 职场：确认会议
A
Are we still on for the 3 p.m. call with the client?
我们跟客户的下午3点电话会议还照常吗？
B
Yes, the client confirmed ten minutes ago.
是的，客户十分钟前确认了。
💳 场景 7 · 货币与支付
A
Do you accept credit cards, or should I pay in cash?
你们收信用卡吗，还是我得付现金？
B
We accept both. There's also a 5% discount for cash.
两种都收。付现金还有5%的折扣。
🧳 场景 8 · 行李遗失求助
A
My suitcase didn't come out on the carousel. What should I do?
我的行李箱没在转盘出来。我该怎么办？
B
Please fill out this form at the lost luggage counter.
请在行李遗失柜台填这张表。
📧 场景 9 · 职场：邮件跟进
A
I haven't heard back from the supplier since Monday.
我从周一起就没收到供应商的回音。
B
Let me give them a quick call to follow up.
我给他们打个电话跟进一下。
👋 场景 10 · 道别
A
Thank you for everything. Have a safe trip home!
谢谢您的一切。祝您回家一路平安！
B
Thanks for hosting me. I'll keep in touch.
谢谢您的招待。我会保持联系。

5 段写作范文

邮件 / 纪要 / 汇报 / 感谢信 / 道歉信
📧 邮件 EMAIL
Subject: Flight Booking Request – Business Trip to Shanghai Dear Travel Team, I am writing to request flight arrangements for my upcoming business trip to Shanghai from July 28 to July 30. I would prefer a morning departure from Beijing on the 28th and a return flight in the evening on the 30th. Economy class is acceptable. Please book a hotel near the conference center and send the itinerary to my email by Friday. Thank you for your assistance. Best regards, Li Hua
主题：机票预订申请——上海商务出差 尊敬的出行团队： 我写信申请为即将到来的上海商务出差（7月28日至30日）安排机票。我希望28日上午从北京出发，30日晚间返程。经济舱即可。请预订会议中心附近的酒店，并于周五前将行程单发送至我的邮箱。感谢您的协助。 此致， 李华
写作技巧：商务邮件应开门见山说明目的，用 "I am writing to..." 开头；时间、舱位、地点等关键信息要具体明确；结尾礼貌致谢。
📝 会议纪要 MINUTES
Meeting Minutes – Team Travel Planning Date: July 23, 2026 Attendees: Marketing Team Key Decisions: 1. The team will visit the Guangzhou trade fair on August 5. 2. Flights and hotels must be booked by July 30. 3. Each member should prepare a 5-minute product presentation. Action Items: - Sarah: confirm booth reservation. - Tom: collect passport copies from all members.
会议纪要——团队出行规划 日期：2026年7月23日 参会人：市场团队 关键决议： 1. 团队将于8月5日参观广州交易会。 2. 机票与酒店须在7月30日前订好。 3. 每位成员需准备5分钟产品介绍。 行动项： - Sarah：确认展位预订。 - Tom：收齐所有成员护照复印件。
写作技巧：会议纪要需记录日期、参会人、决议与行动项；多用被动语态和名词化表达（如 "must be booked"）更显客观正式。
📊 进度汇报 REPORT
Weekly Progress Report This week I completed visits to three key clients in Shenzhen. Two clients signed letters of intent, and one requested a revised quotation. Overall, we are on track to reach 80% of the quarterly target. Next week I will follow up with the pending client and prepare the monthly summary.
每周进度汇报 本周我完成了对深圳三家重点客户的拜访。两家客户签署了意向书，一家要求修订报价。总体来看，我们有望达成季度目标的80%。下周我将跟进待定客户并准备月度总结。
写作技巧：进度汇报用数据说话，先给结果再补细节；用 "on track to" 等短语表达进度符合预期，简洁有力。
💌 感谢信 THANKS
Dear Mr. Smith, Thank you for the warm reception during my visit to your office last week. I truly enjoyed our discussion on future cooperation. Your insights into the local market were invaluable. I look forward to working together and hope to host you in Beijing soon. Sincerely, Li Hua
尊敬的 Smith 先生： 感谢上周到访贵公司时您的热情接待。我十分享受我们关于未来合作的讨论。您对本地市场的见解非常宝贵。期待与您合作，并希望不久能在北京接待您。 此致， 李华
写作技巧：感谢信要具体提到对方的付出（warm reception, insights），并表达未来合作意愿，避免空泛套话。
🙏 道歉信 APOLOGY
Dear Team, I apologize for being late to this morning's meeting. My flight was delayed due to bad weather, and I was unable to notify you earlier. I have reviewed the meeting notes shared by Sarah and will complete my part by tomorrow. Thank you for your understanding. Best, Li Hua
各位好： 我为今早会议迟到致歉。我的航班因天气原因延误，且未能提前通知大家。我已阅读 Sarah 分享的会议纪要，将于明天前完成我负责的部分。感谢大家的理解。 此致， 李华
写作技巧：道歉信结构：致歉 → 解释原因（简洁客观）→ 补救措施 → 致谢。避免过多辩解，重点放在"接下来怎么做"。

2 段 BEC 中级听力

企业职场场景 · 字幕切换 + 语音朗读

🎧 听力 1 · 电话安排商务旅行

Emma:Good morning, Horizon Travel. This is Emma speaking. How may I help you?早上好，Horizon旅行社。我是Emma。有什么可以帮您？

David:Hi Emma, this is David from the sales department. I need to book a trip to Berlin for next Monday.你好Emma，我是销售部的David。我需要订下周一去柏林的行程。

Emma:Certainly, David. Will this be a direct flight or do you prefer a connection?好的David。您要直飞还是接受转机？

David:A direct flight if possible. I also need a hotel close to the exhibition center.尽量直飞。我还需要一家靠近展览中心的酒店。

Emma:Noted. I'll send the itinerary to your email by this afternoon. Anything else?记下了。我会在今天下午前把行程单发到您邮箱。还有别的吗？

David:No, that's all. Thanks for your help.没有了，谢谢你的帮助。

📋 听力任务（点击查看答案）

1. Where is David going?
答：Berlin（柏林）。
2. What kind of flight does he prefer?
答：A direct flight（直飞航班）。
3. What does he need near the hotel?
答：The exhibition center（展览中心）。

🎧 听力 2 · 会议讨论差旅预算

Manager:Let's review the travel budget for the second quarter. We spent 20% more than planned.我们来回顾一下第二季度的差旅预算。我们超支了20%。

Staff:The main increase came from last-minute flight bookings during the trade fair.增长主要来自交易会期间临时预订的机票。

Manager:I see. From now on, please book at least two weeks in advance.我明白了。今后请至少提前两周预订。

Staff:Agreed. We can also use the corporate discount with our partner airline.同意。我们还可以用合作航空公司的企业折扣。

Manager:Good idea. Please summarize this in the monthly report.好主意。请在月度报告里总结一下。

📋 听力任务（点击查看答案）

1. How much over budget did they go?
答：20% more than planned（超支20%）。
2. What caused the increase?
答：Last-minute flight bookings during the trade fair（交易会期间临时订票）。
3. What solution is suggested?
答：Book two weeks in advance + use corporate discount（提前两周订 + 用企业折扣）。
