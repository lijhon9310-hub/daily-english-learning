10 句情景对话

Sure. May I have your name and a contact number?
好的。请留下您的姓名和联系电话。

A
What's today's special?
今天的特色菜是什么？
B
Our chef recommends the grilled lamb with rosemary.
主厨推荐迷迭香烤羊排。
💼 场景 3 · 职场：商务宴请
A
Thank you for joining us for dinner. Please order whatever you like.
感谢您赏光共进晚餐。请随意点菜。
B
That's very kind of you. I'll follow your lead.
您太客气了。我听您安排。

很好喝——稍咸了点，但很有味道。

好主意。选个有荤有素的地方吧。
🛒 场景 6 · 超市买菜

这个食谱还需要别的吗？

📱 场景 7 · 外卖点餐

我点外卖当午饭。你们想吃什么？

清淡点的，沙拉或三明治都行。

5 段写作范文

邮件 / 纪要 / 汇报 / 感谢信 / 道歉信
📧 邮件 EMAIL
Subject: Dinner Invitation – Welcoming Mr. Smith to Beijing Dear Mr. Smith, We are delighted that you will be visiting our Beijing office next week. To celebrate our partnership, we would like to host you for dinner at a local restaurant on Tuesday evening at 7 p.m. The restaurant serves authentic Sichuan cuisine. Please let us know if you have any dietary restrictions. We look forward to your reply. Sincerely, Li Hua
主题：晚宴邀请——欢迎 Smith 先生莅临北京 尊敬的 Smith 先生： 我们非常高兴您下周将到访北京办公室。为庆祝我们的合作，我们诚邀您于周二晚7点在一家本地餐厅共进晚餐。该餐厅提供正宗川菜。如有饮食忌口请告知。期待您的回复。 此致， 李华
写作技巧：邀请邮件要清晰给出时间、地点、事由，并主动询问忌口（dietary restrictions），体现周到与尊重。
📝 会议纪要 MINUTES
Meeting Minutes – Team Building Dinner Date: July 24, 2026 Attendees: Sales Team Key Decisions: 1. A team dinner will be held this Friday at 6:30 p.m. 2. The venue will be a hot-pot restaurant near the office. 3. The budget is capped at 150 yuan per person. Action Items: - Lily: make the reservation for 12 people. - Tom: collect dietary preferences by Thursday.
会议纪要——团队聚餐安排 日期：2026年7月24日 参会人：销售团队 关键决议： 1. 本周五晚6:30举行团队聚餐。 2. 地点选在公司附近的一家火锅店。 3. 人均预算上限150元。 行动项： - Lily：预订12人位。 - Tom：周四前收齐饮食偏好。
写作技巧：团建类纪要同样需记录时间、地点、预算与行动项；预算用具体数字（150 yuan/person）更明确可落地。
📊 进度汇报 REPORT
Weekly Progress Report This week we organized a tasting session for our new ready-to-eat meal line. Over 80 customers participated, and 72% rated the flavors as "very good." The vegetarian options received the highest praise. Based on feedback, we will adjust the salt level before the official launch next month.
每周进度汇报 本周我们为新款即食餐系列组织了试吃活动。80多位客户参与，72%的人给出口味"非常好"的评价。素食款获得最高评价。根据反馈，我们将在次月正式上市前调整咸度。
写作技巧：用百分比和数据支撑结论；以 "based on feedback" 自然引出后续改进动作，逻辑顺畅。
💌 感谢信 THANKS
Dear Mr. Chen, Thank you for the wonderful dinner last night. The dishes were exquisite, and the conversation was even better. I truly enjoyed experiencing the local cuisine with you. I hope to return your hospitality when you visit our city. With gratitude, Li Hua
尊敬的陈先生： 感谢您昨晚的盛情晚宴。菜品精致，相谈甚欢。与您一同体验本地美食令我十分愉快。期待您到访我市时我能尽地主之谊。 此致， 李华
写作技巧：感谢宴请要具体夸赞菜品与氛围，并表达回访意愿（return your hospitality），礼尚往来更显真诚。
🙏 道歉信 APOLOGY
Dear Team, I apologize for the confusion at dinner yesterday. I booked the wrong restaurant, which turned out to be fully reserved, and we had to wait 40 minutes. I should have confirmed the reservation earlier. I will arrange a proper make-up dinner next week. Thank you for your patience. Best, Li Hua
各位好： 为昨晚晚餐的混乱致歉。我订错了餐厅，到店发现已满座，大家等了40分钟。我本应更早确认预订。下周我将重新安排一顿像样的聚餐补偿。感谢大家的耐心。 此致， 李华
写作技巧：道歉信聚焦自身责任，给出具体补救措施（make-up dinner），不过度推卸；结尾致谢缓和气氛。

2 段 BEC 中级听力

企业职场场景 · 字幕切换 + 语音朗读

🎧 听力 1 · 电话预订宴会包间

Receptionist:Good afternoon, Grand Hotel. How can I help you?下午好，大酒店。有什么可以帮您？

Caller:Hi, I'd like to book a private dining room for 12 people next Friday.你好，我想下周五预订一间12人的包间。

Receptionist:Certainly. Will this be a set menu or a buffet?好的。您要套餐还是自助？

Caller:A set menu, please. And we need at least three vegetarian options.套餐。另外我们需要至少三道素食。

Receptionist:Noted. May I have your name and phone number for the reservation?记下了。请留下您的姓名和电话以便预订。

Caller:It's Li Hua, and my number is 138-0000-0000.我叫李华，电话138-0000-0000。

📋 听力任务（点击查看答案）

1. How many people and when?
答：12 people, next Friday（12人，下周五）。
2. Set menu or buffet?
答：A set menu（套餐）。
3. Any special requirement?
答：At least three vegetarian options（至少三道素食）。

🎧 听力 2 · 会议讨论员工食堂餐饮预算

Manager:Let's talk about the staff canteen budget for next quarter.我们来谈谈下季度员工食堂的预算。

Staff:Employees asked for more healthy options, like salads and low-sugar drinks.员工希望有更多健康选择，比如沙拉和低糖饮品。

Manager:Good point. But our catering cost went up 15% this quarter.说得对。但我们本季度餐饮成本上涨了15%。

Staff:We could negotiate a new contract with the supplier to control costs.我们可以和供应商重新谈合同来控制成本。

Manager:Agreed. Please prepare a proposal and we'll review it next week.同意。请准备一份方案，我们下周评审。

📋 听力任务（点击查看答案）

1. What did employees request?
答：More healthy options（更多健康选择）。
2. How much did catering cost rise?
答：15% this quarter（本季度上涨15%）。
3. What's the proposed solution?
答：Negotiate a new supplier contract（与供应商重谈合同）。
