🔤 实用词汇 · 美食文化
gastronomy
/ˌɡæstrəˈnɒmi/
烹饪学，美食学
French gastronomy is renowned for its refined techniques and rich flavors.
💡 gastro（胃）+ nomy（学科）= 关于胃的学科 = 烹饪学
savor
/ˈseɪvər/
品味，细细品尝
Take time to savor each bite of the artisan cheese.
💡 savor = 慢慢享受味道，联想"塞入口中慢慢回味"
culinary
/ˈkʌlɪnəri/
烹饪的，厨房的
She enrolled in a culinary institute to master pastry making.
💡 culin（厨房）+ ary = 与厨房相关的 = 烹饪的
palate
/ˈpælət/
味觉，品尝能力
This wine has a complex palate with notes of blackberry and oak.
💡 palate = 上颚/味觉，联想"拍立特"相机般精确捕捉味道
appetizer
/ˈæpɪtaɪzər/
开胃菜，前菜
The bruschetta served as a delightful appetizer before the main course.
💡 appetite（食欲）+ izer = 激发食欲的东西 = 开胃菜
seasoning
/ˈsiːzənɪŋ/
调味料，调料
A pinch of cumin is the key seasoning in this Moroccan stew.
💡 season（调味/季节）+ ing = 给食物"加季" = 调味料
artisan
/ˈɑːrtɪzən/
手工匠人，手工艺的
The artisan bakery uses traditional methods to sourdough bread.
💡 art（技艺）+ isan = 以技艺为生的人 = 手工匠人
fermentation
/ˌfɜːrmənˈteɪʃən/
发酵
Fermentation gives kimchi its distinctive tangy flavor and health benefits.
💡 ferment（发酵）+ ation = 发酵过程，联想"发酵mentation"
delectable
/dɪˈlektəbl/
令人愉悦的，美味可口的
The delectable chocolate fondant melted perfectly on the tongue.
💡 delect（诱惑）+ able = 能诱惑人的 = 令人垂涎的
pungent
/ˈpʌndʒənt/
刺鼻的，气味浓烈的
The pungent aroma of garlic fills the kitchen when the dish is prepared.
💡 pung（刺）+ ent = 刺鼻的，联想"胖真"浓味扑面而来

✍️ 写作范文 · 美食文化
邮件 · 餐厅预订
Dear Manager, I would like to reserve a table for eight guests on Friday, July 25th at 7:00 PM. We are celebrating a colleague's promotion and would appreciate a private dining area if available. One of our guests has a gluten allergy, so please ensure suitable menu options. Kindly confirm the booking at your earliest convenience. Best regards, Emily Chen
尊敬的经理： 我想在7月25日周五晚上7点为8位客人预订一桌。我们正在庆祝一位同事的升职，如有私人用餐区请优先安排。一位客人有麸质过敏，请确保提供合适的菜单选项。 请在方便时尽早确认预订。 Emily Chen 敬上
✅ 写作技巧：预订邮件需明确时间、人数和特殊需求；语气礼貌但具体；用"would appreciate"表达柔性请求比"need"更得体。
会议纪要 · 食品安全培训
Meeting Minutes — Food Safety Compliance Training Date: July 18, 2026 Attendees: Operations Team, Kitchen Staff, QA Department 1. All kitchen personnel must complete the updated hygiene certification by August 15. 2. Cold storage temperatures must be logged every 4 hours; deviations exceeding 2°C require immediate reporting. 3. Cross-contamination prevention: separate cutting boards for raw meat and vegetables must be color-coded and labeled. 4. Next review meeting scheduled for September 5.
会议纪要 — 食品安全合规培训 日期：2026年7月18日 参会人员：运营团队、厨房员工、质量保证部 1. 所有厨房人员须在8月15日前完成更新的卫生认证。 2. 冷藏温度每4小时须记录一次；偏差超过2°C须立即上报。 3. 交叉污染预防：生肉和蔬菜须使用分开的、颜色编码标注的砧板。 4. 下次评审会议定于9月5日。
✅ 写作技巧：会议纪要用编号条目清晰列出决议；每个条目须包含"谁+做什么+何时完成"三要素；避免模糊表述如"should consider"。
进度汇报 · 新菜单开发
Menu Development Progress Report — Week 3 Completed this week: - Finalized 6 appetizer recipes with cost analysis per serving ($4.20–$7.50). - Conducted taste testing with 12 volunteer panelists; average satisfaction score 8.4/10. In progress: - Main course recipe optimization — currently reducing sodium content by 15% without compromising flavor. - Supplier negotiations for seasonal ingredients (target: 10% cost reduction on local produce). Next week targets: - Complete dessert lineup and finalize full menu pricing structure.
菜单开发进度汇报 — 第3周 本周完成： - 完成6道开胃菜配方及每份成本分析（$4.20–$7.50）。 - 对12位志愿品鉴员进行口味测试；平均满意度8.4/10。 进行中： - 主菜配方优化——在不影响口味的前提下将钠含量降低15%。 - 季节性食材供应商谈判（目标：本地农产品成本降低10%）。 下周目标： - 完成甜品系列并确定完整菜单定价结构。
✅ 写作技巧：进度汇报采用"已完成/进行中/下一步"三段结构；数据量化（人数、评分、百分比）增强说服力；每项用动词开头保持专业节奏。
感谢信 · 餐饮合作伙伴
Dear Chef Rodriguez, Thank you for collaborating with us on the Summer Tasting Event last weekend. Your innovative tapas selection was the highlight of the evening — guests specifically praised the seared scallops with saffron aioli. We received overwhelmingly positive feedback and a 40% increase in reservations for the following week. We look forward to exploring more creative partnerships in the future. Warm regards, The Lakeside Hotel Events Team
尊敬的 Rodriguez 厨师： 感谢您上周末与我们合作举办夏日品鉴活动。您创新的塔帕斯精选是当晚亮点——客人特别称赞了藏红花蒜泥煎扇贝。 我们收到了极其积极的反馈，随后一周预订量增加了40%。期待未来更多创意合作。 Lakeside 酒店活动团队 敬上
✅ 写作技巧：感谢信要具体提及对方贡献（而非泛泛感谢）；附上可量化成果展示合作价值；结尾暗示未来合作意愿维护长期关系。
道歉解释 · 餐食延误
Dear Ms. Thompson, I sincerely apologize for the delay in delivering your catering order on July 18th. Due to an unexpected equipment malfunction in our commercial oven, your dishes were prepared 45 minutes behind schedule. We have since conducted a full maintenance review and implemented backup preparation protocols to prevent such incidents. As a gesture of goodwill, we would like to offer you a 30% discount on your next order. We value your trust and are committed to delivering the service excellence you expect. Yours sincerely, David Park, Operations Director
尊敬的 Thompson 女士： 我真诚地为7月18日配送餐饮订单的延误道歉。由于商用烤箱意外设备故障，您的菜品延迟了45分钟完成。 我们已进行全面维护审查并实施了备用准备流程以防止此类事故。作为善意补偿，我们愿为您提供下次订单30%的折扣。 我们珍视您的信任，致力于提供您期望的卓越服务。 David Park，运营总监 敬上
✅ 写作技巧：道歉信须包含"承认错误+解释原因+已采取补救措施+补偿方案"四要素；避免推卸责任用语如"unfortunately"；补偿方案具体明确（折扣而非笼统"优惠"）。

听力1：餐厅供应链会议

Good morning, everyone. Let's start with the supply chain review for Q3. Our local farm partnerships have delivered consistently this quarter — 95% on-time fulfillment rate across all fresh produce categories. However, the imported specialty items, particularly the truffle oil and aged Parmesan from Italy, have experienced shipping delays averaging eight days due to port congestion. I propose we diversify our sourcing for premium ingredients. We've identified two alternative suppliers in Spain and New Zealand who can provide comparable quality at 12% lower cost, though lead times would be slightly longer. The seasonal menu rollout is scheduled for September 1st. All new recipes must be finalized by August 15th to allow sufficient time for ingredient procurement and staff training. Kitchen managers should submit their ingredient lists to procurement by end of this week. Finally, regarding food waste metrics — our current waste ratio stands at 7.2%, down from 9.8% last quarter. The composting initiative has been effective, but I'd like to see us reach 5% by Q4. Let's discuss actionable steps in the next session.

📋 听力任务

  • Task 1： What is the current on-time fulfillment rate for local farm produce?
  • Task 2： Why have imported specialty items experienced delays?
  • Task 3： What percentage cost reduction do the alternative suppliers offer?
  • Task 4： When must all new recipes be finalized?
  • Task 5： What is the current food waste ratio, and what target is set for Q4?

听力2：食品安全审计反馈

Thank you for joining this follow-up session on our recent food safety audit. Overall, the audit results are encouraging. Our compliance score improved from 82 to 91 out of 100, reflecting significant progress since the last review. The cold chain management scored particularly well — all refrigeration units maintained temperatures within the required 0–5°C range throughout the monitoring period. However, two areas require immediate attention. First, three instances of expired labeling were found in the dry storage section. Products past their use-by date must be removed within 24 hours of expiration — this is non-negotiable. Second, the handwashing station near Station 3 lacks soap dispensers and was observed being bypassed by staff during peak hours. Corrective actions are straightforward: implement a daily label-check protocol at 6 AM before operations begin, and equip all handwashing stations with touchless dispensers plus reminder signage by next Monday. I want to emphasize that food safety is not just regulatory compliance — it's our brand promise to every customer who walks through our doors. Let's treat these corrections as an investment in trust, not just a checklist item.

📋 听力任务

  • Task 1： What was the compliance score improvement from the previous audit?
  • Task 2： What temperature range must refrigeration units maintain?
  • Task 3： What two problems were identified in the audit?
  • Task 4： What corrective action is proposed for expired labeling?
  • Task 5： How does the speaker frame food safety beyond regulation?
