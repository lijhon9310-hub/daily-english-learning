10个实用词汇 · 金融理财

#1
Portfolio
/pɔːrtˈfoʊlioʊ/
投资组合；一组投资资产的集合
She diversified her portfolio to reduce risk across different asset classes.
联想"文件夹(portfolio)"装了各种投资文件 → 投资组合
#2
Diversify
/daɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪ/
多元化；分散投资以降低风险
It's wise to diversify your investments rather than putting all money in one stock.
diverse(多样的) + -ify(使…) → 使投资变得多样 → 分散投资
#3
Dividend
/ˈdɪvɪdend/
股息；公司向股东分配的利润
The company declared a quarterly dividend of $0.50 per share.
divide(分配) + end → 分到最后的利润 → 股息红利
#4
Compound interest
/ˈkɒmpaʊnd ˈɪntrɪst/
复利；利息生利息的计息方式
Compound interest is the eighth wonder of the world — it makes your money grow exponentially.
compound(复合) + interest(利息) → 利滚利 → 复利
#5
Asset allocation
/ˈæset ˌæləˈkeɪʃən/
资产配置；将资金分配到不同资产类别的策略
Proper asset allocation is more important than picking individual stocks.
asset(资产) + allocation(分配) → 如何分配你的钱 → 资产配置
#6
Yield
/jiːld/
收益率；投资产生的收益比率
The bond has a current yield of 4.2%, which is higher than the average savings rate.
联想"产出(yield)" → 投资产出的比率 → 收益率
#7
Inflation
/ɪnˈfleɪʃən/
通货膨胀；物价持续上涨导致货币贬值
With inflation at 3%, your money loses purchasing power if it just sits in a bank.
inflate(膨胀) + -ion → 经济膨胀 → 通货膨胀
#8
Hedge
/hedʒ/
对冲；采取措施降低潜在损失风险
Investors use gold as a hedge against market volatility and currency depreciation.
联想"篱笆(hedge)" → 围篱笆保护自己 → 对冲风险
#9
Liquidity
/lɪˈkwɪdɪti/
流动性；资产快速变现的能力
Cash has the highest liquidity, while real estate is considered a low-liquidity asset.
liquid(液体) + -ity → 流动状态 → 流动性（资产像水一样流动）
#10
ROI
/rɔɪ/ (Return on Investment)
投资回报率；投资收益与投入成本之比
The marketing campaign generated an ROI of 150%, far exceeding our expectations.
R(Return回报) + O(on关于) + I(Investment投资) → 投资回报率

最近股市波动很大。我正考虑把一些资金转到债券上。

Based on your risk tolerance, I'd recommend a balanced portfolio with 60% equities and 40% fixed income.
根据您的风险承受能力，我建议配置一个平衡组合：60%股票和40%固定收益。

投资复盘会议 #10

我们的投资组合今年回报8.2%，跑赢基准指数2个百分点。

5段英文写作范文 · 金融理财场景

邮件 · 投资建议
Dear Ms. Chen, Following our discussion last Thursday, I have prepared a revised investment proposal tailored to your medium-term financial objectives. Given the current market conditions and your risk profile, I recommend reallocating 30% of your equity holdings into dividend-paying blue-chip stocks, which historically provide stable returns during periods of market uncertainty. Additionally, increasing your bond allocation from 20% to 30% would help hedge against potential downside risk. I have attached a detailed breakdown of the proposed asset allocation along with projected yields for each category. Please review the document at your convenience, and we can schedule a follow-up meeting to finalize the strategy. Best regards, David Wang
陈女士您好， 继上周四的讨论之后，我已根据您的中期财务目标准备了修订后的投资建议。考虑到当前市场状况和您的风险偏好，我建议将30%的股票持仓重新配置到支付股息的蓝筹股，这类股票在市场不确定时期历来提供稳定回报。此外，将债券配置从20%提升至30%有助于对冲潜在的下行风险。 我已附上建议资产配置的详细分解以及各类别的预计收益率。请方便时审阅该文件，我们可以安排后续会议以确定最终策略。 诚挚地，王大卫
使用"Following our discussion"开头建立上下文衔接；用"Given..."引出基于条件的建议；附上具体数据增强说服力；结尾用"schedule a follow-up"推动下一步行动。
会议纪要 · 季度财务回顾
Quarterly Financial Review Meeting — Q2 2026 Date: July 10, 2026 Attendees: CFO Lisa Zhang, VP of Operations Mark Chen, Financial Analyst Tom Liu Key Discussion Points: 1. Revenue grew 12% year-over-year, reaching $4.8M, primarily driven by the expansion into the Southeast Asian market. 2. Operating expenses increased by 8%, largely due to the new regional office setup and hiring costs. The CFO noted that these costs are expected to stabilize by Q4. 3. Cash flow remained positive at $1.2M, with liquidity ratio improving from 1.5 to 1.8, indicating stronger short-term financial health. Action Items: - Tom Liu will prepare a detailed cost-benefit analysis for the regional expansion by July 25. - Mark Chen will negotiate with vendors to reduce procurement costs by at least 5%. - Next review meeting scheduled for October 8, 2026.
季度财务回顾会议 — 2026年第二季度 日期：2026年7月10日 参会者：CFO张丽莎、运营副总裁陈马克、财务分析师刘汤姆 关键讨论要点： 1. 收入同比增长12%，达到480万美元，主要由东南亚市场扩张推动。 2. 运营支出增长8%，主要因新区域办公室设立和招聘成本。CFO指出这些成本预计在第四季度趋于稳定。 3. 现金流保持正值120万美元，流动性比率从1.5提升至1.8，表明短期财务健康状况更强。 行动项： - 刘汤姆将在7月25日前准备区域扩张的详细成本效益分析。 - 陈马克将与供应商谈判，至少降低采购成本5%。 - 下次回顾会议定于2026年10月8日。
会议纪要采用结构化格式：先列基本信息，再按"讨论要点"和"行动项"分块；用数字编号增加条理性；每个行动项指定负责人和截止日期；用具体数字（百分比、金额）替代模糊描述。
进度汇报 · 财务系统升级
Subject: Progress Report — Financial Management System Upgrade Dear Director Wu, I am writing to update you on the progress of our financial management system upgrade project, which is currently in its second phase. Completed Milestones: - Phase 1 (Data Migration): Successfully migrated 98% of historical transaction data from the legacy system to the new platform. The remaining 2% involves records from 2010–2012 that require manual validation. - Integration Testing: Core modules (accounts payable, accounts receivable, and general ledger) have passed all functional tests with zero critical defects identified. Current Status: - Phase 2 (User Training): Training sessions have been conducted for 75% of the finance department staff. Feedback surveys show an 85% satisfaction rate regarding the training content and delivery. Pending Risks: - The compliance reporting module may require an additional two weeks for regulatory review. I will keep you informed of any schedule adjustments. Please let me know if you would like a detailed project timeline or wish to discuss any concerns. Respectfully, Sarah Li
主题：进度汇报 — 财务管理系统升级 吴总监您好， 我写信汇报财务管理系统升级项目的进度，项目目前处于第二阶段。 已完成里程碑： - 第一阶段（数据迁移）：成功将98%的历史交易数据从旧系统迁移到新平台。剩余2%涉及2010-2012年记录，需人工验证。 - 集成测试：核心模块（应付账款、应收账款、总账）已通过所有功能测试，未发现关键缺陷。 当前状态： - 第二阶段（用户培训）：已完成财务部门75%员工的培训。反馈调查显示85%的满意度。 待解决风险： - 合规报告模块可能需要额外两周进行监管审查。如有进度调整，我会及时告知。 如需详细项目时间线或讨论任何疑虑，请告知。 敬上，李莎拉
进度汇报按"已完成→当前→待解决"逻辑递进；用百分比和数字量化进度，避免主观描述；主动揭示风险而非隐瞒；结尾提供进一步沟通的渠道，体现专业负责态度。

感谢信要具体而非泛泛：引用实际业绩数据(9.7%)而非只说"做得好"；提及对方的具体贡献（参加季度回顾、耐心过渡）；用"As we move into..."自然过渡到未来展望，避免突兀。
道歉解释 · 报告数据延迟
Dear Mr. Park, I am writing to apologize for the delay in delivering the monthly financial report, which was scheduled for July 5 but was not submitted until July 9. I fully understand the impact this delay may have had on your board presentation and decision-making timeline. The delay was caused by an unexpected discrepancy identified during our reconciliation process between the internal accounting system and the bank statements. Specifically, three large transactions from late June were recorded in different currencies and required manual verification to ensure accurate conversion rates were applied. Rather than submitting a report with potential inaccuracies, we chose to verify every figure thoroughly, which took four additional days. To prevent similar delays in the future, I have implemented the following measures: - Automated currency conversion alerts will be activated in our reconciliation workflow starting this week. - A dedicated review team will be assigned to handle cross-currency transactions proactively each month. - The report delivery schedule has been adjusted to include a two-day buffer for exceptional verification needs. I deeply regret any inconvenience this may have caused and appreciate your understanding. Please let me know if you need any additional clarification regarding the report data. Sincerely, Kevin Zhao Finance Reporting Manager
朴先生您好， 我写信为月度财务报告的延迟致歉，该报告原定7月5日提交，实际延迟至7月9日。我完全理解这一延迟可能对您的董事会演示和决策时间线造成的影响。 延迟原因是对账过程中发现了内部会计系统与银行对账单之间的意外差异。具体而言，六月末的三笔大额交易以不同货币记录，需要人工核验以确保采用准确的汇率。我们选择彻底核实每个数字而非提交可能有误的报告，这额外耗费了四天。 为防止未来类似延迟，我已采取以下措施： - 本周起在对账流程中启用自动化汇率转换提醒。 - 指派专门审核团队每月主动处理跨币种交易。 - 调整报告交付时间表，增加两天的异常核验缓冲期。 我对由此造成的不便深感歉意，感谢您的理解。如需对报告数据的额外说明，请告知。 诚挚地，赵凯文 财务报告经理
道歉信遵循"认错→原因→补救→预防"四步结构；说明原因时避免推诿，强调"选择准确性而非速度"展现专业操守；给出具体可执行的预防措施而非空泛承诺；语气诚恳但不卑微。

2段BEC中级听力材料 · 企业职场场景

🎧 Listening 1: Quarterly Budget Review Meeting

Good morning, everyone. Let's begin the quarterly budget review. I'll start by summarizing our current financial position. As you can see from the dashboard, our total revenue for Q2 reached $12.4 million, which represents a 15% increase compared to the same period last year. This growth was primarily driven by our new product line in the Asian market, which accounted for $3.2 million of the total. However, our operating expenses also rose significantly. Marketing costs increased by 22% due to the launch campaign, and R&D spending grew by 18% as we invested in next-generation features. Net profit margin therefore declined slightly from 14% to 12.8%. Looking ahead to Q3, I propose we implement cost optimization measures across three areas. First, we should renegotiate our vendor contracts — our current agreements with cloud service providers expire in August, and market rates have dropped by approximately 10%. Second, I recommend consolidating our regional marketing budgets into a centralized fund to eliminate duplicated efforts. Third, we need to prioritize R&D projects based on their projected ROI rather than continuing all initiatives simultaneously. If we execute these measures effectively, I estimate we can restore our net profit margin to 15% by the end of Q3. I'd like to hear your thoughts before we finalize the action plan.
听第一段后回答：1) Q2总收入是多少？ 2) 净利润率从多少降到多少？ 3) 提出了哪三个成本优化措施？

🎧 Listening 2: Client Portfolio Review Discussion

Thank you for joining this portfolio review session, Ms. Reed. Let me walk you through the performance of your investments over the past six months. Your total portfolio value currently stands at $2.6 million, up from $2.4 million at the beginning of the year — that's a 8.3% return, which outperforms the benchmark index by 1.5 percentage points. The equity portion of your portfolio delivered the strongest performance, particularly the technology sector holdings, which grew by 14%. However, this concentration also exposed you to higher volatility during the March market correction, when your portfolio temporarily dipped by 6%. On the fixed income side, your bond allocation provided stable returns at 3.8%, serving as an effective hedge during the equity downturn. The newly added emerging market bonds, though, underperformed at 2.1% due to currency fluctuations in Southeast Asia. Based on this review, I'd suggest two adjustments. First, reducing your technology sector weighting from 35% to 25% would lower volatility without sacrificing long-term growth potential, as we'd reallocate to healthcare and consumer staples. Second, increasing your alternative investments — specifically infrastructure funds — from 5% to 10% could provide both diversification and inflation protection, since infrastructure revenues are often linked to inflation indices. Would you like me to prepare a revised allocation proposal incorporating these changes?
听第二段后回答：1) 投资组合总价值从多少增长到多少？ 2) 科技板块权重建议从多少降到多少？ 3) 为什么建议增加基础设施基金配置？

Portfolio value投资组合价值
Benchmark index基准指数
Volatility波动性
Market correction市场调整（下跌）
Fixed income固定收益
