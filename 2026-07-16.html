✍️ 英文写作范文 — 旅行出行
📧 邮件 — 请求出差预算审批
Dear Ms. Chen,

I am writing to request budget approval for my upcoming business trip to Tokyo scheduled for August 3–5. The estimated costs are as follows: round-trip airfare ¥4,200, accommodation (2 nights) ¥1,800, and local transportation ¥600, totaling ¥6,600.

This trip is essential for finalizing the partnership agreement with Sato Corp, which we have been negotiating since Q1. I would appreciate your prompt approval so I can proceed with the bookings.

Best regards,
Li Wei
陈女士您好，我写信请求批准我8月3-5日东京出差的预算。预估费用：往返机票¥4,200、住宿（2晚）¥1,800、本地交通¥600，合计¥6,600。此行对敲定与Sato公司的合作至关重要，恳请尽快审批以便预订。
✏️ 写作技巧：预算审批邮件要条目清晰（列出明细）、说明必要性（业务理由）、结尾用 "I would appreciate your prompt approval" 正式催促。

✏️ 写作技巧：会议纪要用编号分节（Logistics / Agenda / Action Items），行动项明确人名和截止日期。
📊 进度汇报 — 出差途中汇报
Dear Team,

I'm writing from the Hilton Bangkok to provide a mid-trip update. Day one went smoothly: the product demo with ThaiMart's procurement team was well received — they particularly appreciated the real-time analytics dashboard. However, they raised concerns about pricing for the enterprise tier, which we'll address in tomorrow's negotiation session.

On the logistics side, everything has been on schedule with no travel disruptions. I will send a full post-trip report upon return on Thursday.

Best,
Li Wei
团队各位，我从曼谷希尔顿发来出差中期汇报。第一天进展顺利：ThaiMart采购团队对产品演示反响良好，尤其认可实时分析仪表盘。但他们对企业版定价有顾虑，明天谈判中处理。物流方面一切按计划无延误。周四返回后将提交完整出差报告。
✏️ 写作技巧：进度汇报分两部分——业务进展（正面成果+待解决问题）和后勤状态，结尾预告下一步。
🙏 感谢信 — 感谢接待方
Dear Mr. Sato,

I would like to express my sincere gratitude for your hospitality during my visit to Tokyo last week. The thoughtful arrangement of both the office tour and the traditional dinner at that remarkable izakaya made my trip not only productive but also culturally enriching.

Our discussions on the partnership framework were constructive, and I am confident we will reach a mutually beneficial agreement soon. I look forward to welcoming you to Shanghai when you visit next quarter.

Warm regards,
Li Wei
佐藤先生，衷心感谢您上周在我东京访问期间的盛情接待。您精心安排的办公参观和居酒屋传统晚宴让此行既高效又文化丰富。关于合作框架的讨论很有建设性，我相信我们很快将达成互利协议。期待下季度您来访上海时接待您。
✏️ 写作技巧：感谢信要具体（提及具体活动），表达双向期待（期待对方来访），语气 warm 而非 cold formal。
🙇 道歉解释 — 行程变更通知
Dear Ms. Park,

I sincerely apologize for the unexpected change to our meeting schedule. Due to a flight cancellation caused by severe weather, I will now arrive in Seoul on Wednesday afternoon instead of Tuesday morning as originally planned.

To minimize the impact on your team, I propose we reschedule our session to Wednesday at 4 PM or Thursday at 10 AM — whichever works better for you. I have already adjusted my itinerary accordingly.

I understand this is inconvenient and truly regret the disruption. Please let me know your preferred time.

Apologies again,
Li Wei
朴女士，因航班因恶劣天气取消，我深表歉意不得不变更会面时间——改为周三下午抵达首尔而非原计划的周二上午。为减少对您团队的影响，建议将会议改到周三下午4点或周四上午10点。我已相应调整行程。理解这会造成不便，请告知您方便的时间。再次致歉。
✏️ 写作技巧：道歉邮件三段式——1) 陈述原因（客观因素如天气），2) 提出补救方案（给选择题），3) 再次致歉+请求确认。

听力 1：Corporate Travel Policy Update（企业出差政策更新）
Good morning, everyone. I'd like to inform you of several updates to our corporate travel policy, effective from August 1st. First, all international business trips must now be approved by your department head at least two weeks in advance. This replaces the previous one-week requirement and gives us more time to negotiate better airfare rates. Second, the per diem allowance for overseas destinations has been revised. For Tier 1 cities — that includes London, New York, Tokyo, and Singapore — the daily rate increases from $80 to $95. For Tier 2 destinations, the rate remains at $65 per day. Third, we are introducing a mandatory travel insurance policy. All employees traveling abroad on company business will be covered under our new global insurance plan. The premium is fully paid by the company — there is no cost to you — but you must register through the HR portal before each trip. Finally, expense reports must now be submitted within five business days of your return, not the previous ten-day window. Late submissions will not be processed until the next quarter. If you have any questions, please contact the Travel Coordination Office. Thank you.

听力 2：Airport Lounge Access Negotiation（机场贵宾厅准入谈判）
Let me outline the current situation regarding our corporate lounge access agreements. We currently hold memberships with two lounge networks: SkyPort lounges, which cover 28 airports across Europe and Asia, and ComfortZone, which operates in 15 airports primarily in North America. Our annual fee for SkyPort is $42,000, and for ComfortZone it's $28,000. The procurement team has been negotiating with a third provider, GlobalRest, which offers coverage at 52 airports worldwide — essentially combining the reach of both our existing providers. Their proposed annual corporate rate is $55,000, which represents a potential saving of $15,000 compared to maintaining both current memberships. However, there are two considerations. First, GlobalRest requires a minimum three-year contract commitment, whereas our existing agreements are renewed annually. Second, their lounges in three key locations — Heathrow, Narita, and LAX — are smaller than those of our current providers, with limited meeting room facilities. Our recommendation is to proceed with GlobalRest for a three-year term, but to negotiate a clause allowing us to retain SkyPort access at Heathrow and Narita as a supplementary arrangement. This would add approximately $8,000 per year but ensure our executives have adequate meeting space at those critical hubs. I'd like your feedback before we finalize the decision. Please share your thoughts by Friday.
