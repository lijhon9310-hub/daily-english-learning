📚 实用词汇 · Vocabulary
#01
artificial intelligence
/ˌɑːrtɪfɪʃl ɪnˈtelɪdʒəns/
人工智能 — 计算机系统模拟人类智能的能力
AI is transforming how businesses analyze customer data and make decisions.
缩写AI人人知，artificial=人造的，intelligence=智能，合起来就是"人造的智能"
#02
algorithm
/ˈælɡərɪðəm/
算法 — 解决问题的一系列有序步骤或规则
The search engine uses a complex algorithm to rank web pages.
联想algebra(代数)+rithm，数学里的"计算步骤"
#03
cloud computing
/klaʊd kəmˈpuːtɪŋ/
云计算 — 通过互联网提供计算资源和存储服务
Many startups rely on cloud computing to reduce infrastructure costs.
cloud=云，computing=计算，数据不在本地而在"云端"，就像把东西存到天上的仓库
#04
cybersecurity
/ˌsaɪbərˈsɪkjʊrɪti/
网络安全 — 保护计算机系统和网络免受攻击的措施
Investing in cybersecurity is no longer optional for modern enterprises.
cyber=网络的，security=安全，合起来就是"网络的安全防护"
#05
blockchain
/ˈblɒkʃeɪn/
区块链 — 分布式、不可篡改的数字记录技术
Blockchain technology ensures transparent and tamper-proof transaction records.
block=区块，chain=链，一个个"区块"像链条一样连接，不可断裂=不可篡改
#06
machine learning
/məˈʃiːn ˈlɜːrnɪŋ/
机器学习 — 让计算机从数据中自动学习规律的技术
Machine learning models can predict market trends with remarkable accuracy.
machine=机器，learning=学习，机器自己在"学习"，不用人手把手教
#07
digital transformation
/ˈdɪdʒɪtl trænsfərˈmeɪʃən/
数字化转型 — 企业用数字技术彻底改变业务模式的过程
Our CEO announced a five-year digital transformation roadmap last quarter.
digital=数字的，transformation=转型，传统企业变成"数字化企业"的过程
#08
data analytics
/ˈdeɪtə ænəˈlɪtɪks/
数据分析 — 对数据进行检查和解读以发现有用信息的过程
Data analytics helped us identify a 30% increase in user engagement.
data=数据，analytics=分析学，把数据"解剖"来找价值
#09
automation
/ˌɔːtəˈmeɪʃən/
自动化 — 用技术代替人工完成重复性任务的过程
Process automation reduced our invoice processing time by 80%.
auto=自动，mation=动作过程，让事情"自动发生"不用人操心
#10
Internet of Things
/ˈɪntərnet əv θɪŋz/
物联网 — 日常物品通过网络互联并交换数据的概念
IoT devices in our warehouse monitor temperature and humidity in real time.
Internet=互联网，Things=物品，万物皆可联网，冰箱、灯泡也能"上网聊天"

🎧 BEC中级听力 · Listening

🎙️ Listening 1: 云服务采购决策会议

场景：公司管理层讨论云服务供应商选择，涉及成本、安全和迁移策略

Cloud Service Vendor Selection Meeting
BEC Business English · Intermediate Level
Good morning, everyone. Today we need to make a final decision on our cloud service provider. After six months of evaluation, we have two candidates: AWS and Microsoft Azure. Let me summarize the key findings from our technical assessment. First, regarding cost structure. AWS offers a pay-as-you-go model with estimated annual spending of $480,000 based on our current workload. Azure's proposal comes at $520,000 annually but includes premium support and additional security features bundled into the package. Second, cybersecurity compliance. Both providers meet ISO 27001 and SOC 2 standards. However, Azure has a dedicated compliance team for the Asia-Pacific region, which aligns better with our regulatory requirements in China and Southeast Asia. Third, migration complexity. Our current infrastructure runs on Microsoft SQL Server and SharePoint. Migrating to Azure would require minimal reconfiguration, while an AWS migration would involve significant database restructuring and potential downtime of two to three weeks. Considering the total cost of ownership, regulatory alignment, and migration risk, I recommend we proceed with Microsoft Azure as our primary cloud provider, with a phased migration starting in Q3. Are there any objections or additional points to consider?
各位早上好。今天我们需要对云服务供应商做出最终决定。经过六个月的评估，我们有两个候选方案：AWS和微软Azure。让我总结一下技术评估的关键发现。 首先，关于成本结构。AWS提供按需付费模式，根据当前工作负载估算年支出为48万美元。Azure方案年费52万美元，但包含高级支持和打包的额外安全功能。 其次，网络安全合规性。两家供应商都符合ISO 27001和SOC 2标准。不过Azure有亚太地区专门的合规团队，这更符合我们在中国和东南亚的监管要求。 第三，迁移复杂度。我们当前基础设施运行在微软SQL Server和SharePoint上。迁移到Azure只需最小化重新配置，而AWS迁移将涉及重大的数据库重构和两到三周的潜在停机时间。 综合考虑总拥有成本、监管契合度和迁移风险，我建议选择微软Azure作为我们的主要云服务商，第三季度开始分阶段迁移。有任何反对意见或需要补充的吗？

🎙️ Listening 2: AI产品发布策略讨论

场景：产品团队讨论AI智能助手产品的发布时机、市场定位和竞争策略

AI Product Launch Strategy Discussion
BEC Business English · Intermediate Level
Thanks for joining this urgent meeting, team. We need to finalize our launch strategy for the AI Smart Assistant before the competitor announced their similar product yesterday at the Tech Summit. Let me present three potential approaches. Option A is an accelerated launch — we release the beta version within four weeks, targeting early adopters in the enterprise sector. This gives us first-mover advantage, but the risk is that our product may lack sufficient testing, and negative early reviews could damage our brand reputation. Option B is a standard timeline launch in October, allowing two more months for quality assurance and user testing. This reduces risk but means we enter the market after our competitor has already established presence and captured initial market share. Option C is a strategic delay — we hold the full launch until Q1 2027 but release a limited preview to selected enterprise clients next month. This lets us gather real-world feedback while keeping market interest alive through controlled exposure. From a financial perspective, Option A could generate $2 million in initial revenue but carries a 40% risk of post-launch issues requiring emergency patches. Option B projects $3.5 million in first-year revenue with only 15% risk of significant bugs. Option C forecasts $5 million over 18 months with the lowest risk profile at 8%. My recommendation is Option C. It balances market timing with product quality and gives us the data we need to refine features before full deployment. I'd like your input before we present this to the board on Friday.
感谢团队参加这次紧急会议。我们需要在竞争对手昨天在科技峰会上宣布类似产品之前，敲定AI智能助手的发布策略。 我提出三种可能的方案。方案A是加速发布——在四周内推出beta版本，面向企业领域的早期用户。这给我们先发优势，但风险是产品可能缺乏充分测试，负面早期评价可能损害品牌声誉。 方案B是标准时间线发布在10月，允许再多两个月进行质量保证和用户测试。这降低了风险但意味着我们在竞争对手已建立市场地位并抢占初始市场份额之后才进入市场。 方案C是战略性延迟——我们将全面发布推迟到2027年第一季度，但下月向精选企业客户发布有限预览。这让我们收集真实反馈，同时通过可控曝光保持市场兴趣。 从财务角度看，方案A可能产生200万美元初始收入，但带有40%的发布后需要紧急补丁的风险。方案B预计第一年收入350万美元，仅有15%的重大漏洞风险。方案C预测18个月内500万美元收入，风险最低仅8%。 我推荐方案C。它在市场时机和产品质量之间取得平衡，并给我们全量部署前优化功能所需的数据。我希望在周五向董事会汇报前听听大家的意见。
