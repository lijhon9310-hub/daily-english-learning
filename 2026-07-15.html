📚 10个实用词汇 — 医疗健康
symptom
/ˈsɪmptəm/
n. 症状；征兆
Symptoms of the flu include fever, cough, and body aches.
联想 sign（标志）→ 症状是疾病的"信号"
prescription
/prɪˈskrɪpʃən/
n. 处方；药方
The doctor gave me a prescription for antibiotics.
pre(预先)+script(写)→ 预先写好的用药指示
diagnosis
/ˌdaɪəɡˈnɒsɪs/
n. 诊断；判断
Early diagnosis can significantly improve treatment outcomes.
dia(通过)+gnosis(知识)→ 通过知识判断病情
chronic
/ˈkrɒnɪk/
adj. 慢性的；长期的
Chronic diseases like diabetes require long-term management.
chron(时间)→ 与时间长期相伴的病
immunity
/ɪˈmjuːnɪti/
n. 免疫力；免疫
Vaccination helps build immunity against infectious diseases.
immune(免疫的)→ 免疫系统守护健康
rehabilitation
/ˌriːəˌbɪlɪˈteɪʃən/
n. 康复；恢复
Rehabilitation after surgery usually takes several weeks.
re(再次)+habilit(能力)→ 重新恢复能力
allergic
/əˈlɜːdʒɪk/
adj. 过敏的；对…过敏的
I'm allergic to penicillin, so I need alternative medication.
allergy(过敏)→ 身体对某些物质的"过度反应"
pharmacy
/ˈfɑːməsi/
n. 药房；药店
You can pick up your medication at the pharmacy on the ground floor.
pharm(药物)→ 处理药物的地方
vital signs
/ˈvaɪtəl saɪnz/
n. 生命体征（血压、心率等）
Nurses check vital signs every four hours for ICU patients.
vital(关键的)→ 维持生命的关键指标
preventive
/prɪˈventɪv/
adj. 预防性的；预防的
Preventive care includes regular screenings and health check-ups.
prevent(预防)→ 防患于未然
💬 10句实用对话 — 医疗健康场景
🏥 场景：预约门诊
I'd like to make an appointment with Dr. Smith for a general check-up.
我想预约Smith医生做一次常规体检。
🏥 场景：描述症状
I've been experiencing persistent headaches and occasional dizziness for about two weeks.
我最近两周一直持续性头痛，偶尔头晕。
🏥 场景：药房取药
Could you fill this prescription for me? And are there any side effects I should watch for?
能帮我配这个处方吗？有什么需要注意的副作用吗？
🏥 场景：了解检查结果
What does my blood test report show? Is there anything abnormal?
我的血液检查报告显示什么？有异常吗？
🏥 场景：职场健康保险
Our company's health insurance plan covers annual physical exams and dental check-ups.
我们公司的医疗保险计划覆盖年度体检和牙科检查。
🏥 场景：请假看病
I need to take a sick day tomorrow. I've got a fever and my doctor advised rest.
明天我需要请病假。我发烧了，医生建议休息。
🏥 场景：讨论预防措施
It's important to get regular screenings — early detection saves lives.
定期筛查很重要——早发现能救命。
🏥 场景：关心同事健康
You look a bit under the weather. Have you considered seeing a doctor?
你看起来不太舒服，考虑去看医生了吗？
🏥 场景：健身与健康
Maintaining a balanced diet and exercising regularly are key to long-term wellness.
保持均衡饮食和规律运动是长期健康的关键。
🏥 场景：住院与探望
My colleague is in hospital for knee surgery. Let's send a card and visit this weekend.
我的同事因膝盖手术住院了。我们寄张卡片，周末去探望吧。

📧 邮件：向同事请假看病
Dear Sarah,

I am writing to inform you that I need to take a sick day today. I have been experiencing a persistent fever and severe headache since last night, and my doctor has advised me to rest at home for at least 24 hours.

I have already informed the project team and delegated my urgent tasks to David. I will monitor my emails periodically and respond to any critical matters. I expect to return to work tomorrow, pending my doctor's clearance.

Please let me know if there is anything urgent that requires my immediate attention.

Best regards,
Emily
亲爱的Sarah，我写信是想通知您我今天需要请病假。昨晚开始我一直发烧并有严重的头痛，医生建议我至少在家休息24小时。我已经通知了项目团队，并将紧急任务委托给了David。我会定期查看邮件并回复关键事项。预计明天恢复上班，取决于医生的许可。如果有任何紧急事项需要我立即处理，请告知。祝好，Emily
邮件请假要简洁明了：说明症状+医生建议+工作交接+预期返回时间，体现责任心。

📝 道歉解释：错过健康培训会议
Dear Mr. Thompson,

I sincerely apologize for missing the mandatory workplace health and safety training session scheduled on July 14. This was entirely my responsibility, and I understand the importance of compliance with our company's health protocols.

The reason for my absence was an unexpected family emergency — my father was admitted to hospital that morning, and I had to accompany him urgently. I should have notified you immediately rather than only informing HR later that afternoon. This was an oversight I will not repeat.

I have already registered for the next available session on July 22 and have reviewed the training materials online. I will ensure full preparation before attending.

I apologize again for any inconvenience this may have caused to the team and the training schedule.

Respectfully,
James Wilson
亲爱的Thompson先生，我真诚地为缺席7月14日的强制职场健康与安全培训会议道歉。这完全是我的责任，我理解遵守公司健康规程的重要性。缺席原因是突发家庭紧急情况——我父亲那天早上被送进医院，我不得不紧急陪同。我应该立即通知您，而不是直到下午才告知HR。这是我不会再犯的疏忽。我已经注册了7月22日的下一次培训，并在线学习了培训材料。我会确保在参加前做好充分准备。再次为给团队和培训日程造成的不便道歉。尊敬的，James Wilson
道歉信结构：直接道歉→说明原因（简洁客观）→承认应做的正确做法→提出补救措施→再次致歉，体现诚意和专业。
